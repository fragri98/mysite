About Me

Hello there, I'm Francesca Grimaldi and I am a Full Stack Developer from Rome, Italy.

I'm an expert in front-end and back-end development. Front-end (client side) is what you see on your browser. It includes the web-page, the layout, different styles, images etc. It is the business logic behind every website. On the other hand, back-end (server side) is like a central location responsible for storing all of your site and user data.
Although I develop both client and server software, I prefer front-end because it allows me to free my creativity and, at the same time, show off my IT skills.
